I'm David Joloko, a finance and crypto professional based in Ontario, Canada with a background in financial markets and crypto. Before going back to school, I built real experience in the finance and crypto space, which means I understand the business context behind the data, not just the tooling.

I'm now completing my Master of Business Analytics & AI (MBAI) at Ontario Tech University and applying that domain knowledge to visualization, exploratory analysis, and real-world data problems.

Most analysts know the tools. I also know the market. That edge shows up in how I frame problems, choose the right data, and communicate what I find.
